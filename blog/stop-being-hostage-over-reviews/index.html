Demanding Airbnb to remove bad reviews

A simple guide to guide you on how to effectively ask for a review to be removed.

According to the Airbnb's Review Policy, "You are not allowed to incentivize positive reviews, to use the threat of a negative review to manipulate a desired outcome, or to influence another’s review with the promise of compensation". Follow the following steps to maximize your chances to have a bad review removed.

1. Try to not comunicate with guests outside Airbnb's platform.

Moderators have full access to Airbnb's message system. Centralizing all communications on Airbnb's platform will facilitate the review process.

2. Shift important verbal matters to written communication.

Real life happens and we're just not able to keep all communication on Airbnb's platform. Just don't deal with sensitive and critical matters over the phone. Phone calls are not evidence.

3. Document everything you can (with quality data)

Make a timeline of the facts, attach screenshots and point to specific messages where you feel like you were threatened.

4. Take [good] pictures

You can also attach pictures to the review removal request. Keep in mind the reviewer didn't go to your place, your pictures must contextualize with enought components to build a case. Also, don't be lazy, take good pictures (no makeup, though).

5. Use the following template

Dear Airbnb reviewer,

I recently had a guest who used his reviewing powers to manipulate a desired outcome.
The guest review is biased, and I would like to dispute it.

According to the Airbnb's Review Policy (https://www.airbnb.ca/help/article/2673/airbnbs-review-policy).
Topic 2, paragraph 2 attests that:
  " Guests are not allowed to incentivize positive reviews,
    to use the threat of a negative review to manipulate a desired outcome,
    or to influence another’s review with the promise of compensation. "

[ELABORATE]

I am also attaching some proofs and, according to the Review Policy, I demand the refered review to be removed.
